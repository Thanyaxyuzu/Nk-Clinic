About Our Clinic

Welcome to NK Clinic, located in Banchang, Thailand. We provide comprehensive health checkups and a range of personalized treatments to support your well-being. Learn more about our doctors and specialized services below.

Our Services

ENT Examination & Consultation

Our clinic provides a wide range of specialized ENT services, including:

  • Assessment and treatment of vertigo and balance issues
  • Treatment of allergic rhinitis, hay fever, and sinusitis
  • Treatment of neck mass and thyroid conditions
  • Snoring and sleep apnea management
  • Hearing evaluation and hearing aids
  • Treatment of ear diseases and hearing problems

Bone and Joint Care

Expert diagnosis and treatment for bone and joint conditions, including:

  • Treatment of bone and joint diseases
  • Office syndrome management
  • Treatment of osteoporosis
  • Treatment of gouty arthritis
  • Treatment of tendinitis

Blood Test & Health Checkup

We provide comprehensive blood checkups including:

  • Complete blood count
  • Kidney and liver function tests
  • Blood sugar and thyroid function
  • Food and inhalant allergy tests
  • Anti-HIV screening
